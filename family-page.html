Her Story
Margaret grew up in St. Paul, the youngest of four sisters. She spent thirty years as a piano teacher and could play anything by ear. She and her husband Robert raised their family in Highland Park, tending the same garden every summer for forty years. She's proud, funny, and fiercely loyal — and she never forgets a face, even when names slip away. Ask her about the 1965 World Series and she'll talk for twenty minutes.
